Destination

Destination is a walking simulator that aims to create a feeling of solitude, somberness, and eventual redemption for the player.

You play as a lost soul who is in search of his body. The player begins alone in a dark forest, with the only source of light coming from a mausoleum. Upon entering the building you stumble and fall into the depths of the catacomb, with the only way to get out by continuing to go deeper and deeper.
