Rocket Man

This is the project started in 2017 Fall. It is a project that selects the target audience with opinions that are contrary to me and delivers my message to the person. Assuming that the person I choose can play this game, I have to convince him. I chose North Korea Kim Jong-un to convey a message that his missile launch should be stopped. I wanted to let him know that if he continued to fire the missile, the damage would eventually come back.
